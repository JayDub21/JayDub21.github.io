About Me

Raised in Abilene, Texas, I was introduced to the great outdoors by my parents at a very young age. I was able to observe nature through the lens of a curious child, which fostered my love and passion for land conservation as I grew older.

My hobbies include: Riding horses, hunting, fishing, and working at our family farm.
